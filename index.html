The Person

Author. Builder.
Dreamer of cities.

Subir Goswami is an author, entrepreneur, and Certified AI-Driven Professional from Kolkata, India — a city whose rivers, monsoons, and labyrinthine lanes have profoundly shaped his imagination.

"Every story I write carries the weight of Kolkata's evenings — the smell of rain on old stone, the sound of bells across the Hooghly."

His debut novel When the Hooghly Whispered Your Name is a romantic literary work exploring love, memory, and the emotional landscapes of living cities.

Beyond literature, Subir is the creator of KaliNova AI — engineered to help businesses grow through intelligent automation, digital strategy, and smart technology. He has also led brand storytelling for landmark Kolkata venues including Cafe Waterloo, Zero Lounge, and LYL Music Pub.
