Who I Am

Started in the field.
Never left it.

From pasting billboards across Bengal to building AI ventures — every chapter has been hands-on.

Subir Goswami Certified AI-Driven Professional · CMO

Subir Goswami started where most marketers never go — on the ground. Negotiating outdoor ad spaces across East India. Sitting across tables with FMCG brand managers who wanted results, not presentations. Learning, the hard way, that great marketing is never about the budget. It's about the idea behind it.

"I've spent 14 years making brands impossible to ignore. Now I'm doing the same thing with AI — and the results are something else entirely."— Subir Goswami

Over 14 years, he rose from Account Manager to Chief Marketing Officer — leading multi-state campaigns, commanding media spends across 10+ states, and building teams from scratch. When AI arrived, he didn't just observe it. He got formally certified by be10X, co-founded KaliNova AI, and launched Upnova Institute to help others make the same leap.

He also writes. Three published books on Amazon. One novel born from Kolkata's midnight streets. Two that teach what he's learned about branding and digital business. Because for Subir, deep thinking and sharp execution have always gone hand in hand.
