About

Subir Goswami is an author and entrepreneur from Kolkata, India. His work explores the intersection of storytelling, artificial intelligence, and modern digital culture.

His debut novel When the Hooghly Whispered Your Name reflects his deep fascination with love, memory, and the emotional landscapes of cities.

He is also the creator of KaliNova AI, an AI-powered concept focused on helping businesses grow through intelligent automation and digital strategy.
